About the data:

USA Trade® Online, created in partnership with the US Census Bureau, is a primary source for US import and export merchandise data. Current and cumulative statistics are available on a monthly, annual, and year-to-date basis. Trade statistics are reported using either the Harmonized System (HS) up to the 10-digit level and/or the NAICS commodity classification codes up to the 6-digit level.

The following trade data is available:

  • Quantity, Value
  • Port Level Detail
  • Method of Transportation
  • Shipping Weight
  • Market Level Ranking
